We are excited for you to join us on 08-Jun-26!

With your first day fast approaching, now is a great time to visit your [preboarding journeys](https://edbz.login.us2.oraclecloud.com/) to verify all of your preboarding tasks are complete. Please see below for information and details in preparation for your first day.

What to expect:

What to bring
You will need to have your valid government-issued picture ID such as a passport or driver license in order to complete registration, pick up your TI badge and laptop on your first day. You do not need to bring a bag for your TI laptop as one will be provided for you.

The attire for the intern welcome event is business casual. We strongly recommend you wear comfortable shoes for the day.

What to expect when you are here
You will need to arrive at the Dallas Support Building located at [13020 TI BLVD, Dallas, TX, 75243](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ozYn8W1LPBRMUaH29) between 7:30-8:30 a.m. CT. Traffic in the morning can vary, please make sure you allow enough time for yourself.
You will park in the Dallas Support Building parking lot and arrive at the lobby to complete registration and pick-up your IT badge and laptop.

After you pick up your TI badge and laptop, the TI shuttle will take you to the North Campus SC Building for the welcome event in the main auditorium. During the first day, you will connect with other TI interns and hear from current TIers. Breakfast and lunch will be provided for you during your welcome event.

In the afternoon, you will complete an IT session showing you how to set up your laptop and access the TI network. At the conclusion of the event, you will be shuttled back to the Dallas Support Building.

What to do after the event
Your supervisor will reach out to you prior to your first day to discuss a time and place to meet at your assigned TI location on day two. If you have not finalized meet up plans with your supervisor, please do so before your intern welcome event.

During your first week, you will need to complete TI's new hire orientation training through the myLearning system. If your role requires any additional training, you will receive an email with more information.


Still have questions?
Please visit [HR Connect](https://texasinstrumentshr.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/815) for more information about getting started at TI.

We are excited to have you join us!
